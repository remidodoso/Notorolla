Exhibit — Tervik 1.2

The 3-op FM instrument with envelopes extracted into their own Envelope section (Env 1 · Env 2 · Env 3); push 1 → 2 / 1 → 3 to copy Env 1's settings (the target flashes). 1.2: the Algorithm control is now a backlit-LCD picker — a lit amber/green glass showing the FM operator graph (numbered boxes + connection lines + carrier output bus, TX81Z-style, dark-on-lit), driven by a plain rotary encoder (unlimited turn, wraps 1→2→3→4→1). It sits at the left of the Routing sub. TX81Z drawing convention — outline boxes, plain lines (no arrows), carriers on a solid output bar; two modulators onto one carrier converge into the "Y". Meta Algo size scales the graph clear of the caption. Tervik's "Feedback" isn't per-operator (it brightens Ops 2 & 3's wave), so the slider is renamed Shape and no operator carries a feedback loop. No Filter/Effects — the spectrum shows its gaps (hue = role).
