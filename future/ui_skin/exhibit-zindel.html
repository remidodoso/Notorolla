Exhibit — Zindel 1.0

The drawbar additive organ, with the drawbar widget in two versions (meta Drawbars): plain — a neat group of ordinary sliders — or tabs — white/black numbered pull-tabs on chrome stems, 0–8 registration, 9-position clicks, packed as a manual (white = powers-of-two harmonics 1·2·4·8, black = the rest). up = louder on both. Acceleration takes the green filter-role slot (the "filter substitute") — band tab "Motion" to stay honest. No LFO/Effects → the role-hue spectrum shows its gaps.
